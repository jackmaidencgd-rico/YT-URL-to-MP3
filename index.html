❓ FAQ

Can I use this for any YouTube video?

No. Only use for videos containing royalty-free, Creative Commons, or publicly available music that you have permission to download.

Is this legal?

Using this tool is only legal for content you have permission to download. Downloading copyrighted content without permission violates copyright law.

What metadata can be extracted?

Video title, artist name, album name, duration, and file name are extracted and can be used to tag the MP3 file.

How long does conversion take?

Conversion typically takes 30-120 seconds depending on the video length and your internet speed.

What if the download doesn't work?

Check your browser console (F12) for errors. Ensure pop-ups aren't blocked. Try a different video.
